Hyde + Hyde Architects are a progressive award winning architecture practice with work across the UK and Europe.


For more than a decade we have been working with discerning clients across the UK and abroad to conceive, create, and express deeply thoughtful buildings.

Our work has been featured in the Sunday Times, Guardian, Daily Telegraph, Grand Designs Magazine and has won numerous local and national awards.

The start of something beautiful…

Many of our clients have been with us for years. Long relationships are a testament to our service and commitment.

People

To ensure quality and consistency – our team work closely with our clients. You can expect to see the same faces from initial design to work on site.

Process

Each project for us is unique and reflects an understanding of the varied circumstances of our clients. Architectural designs are developed using innovative materials, advanced structural strategies and sustainable technologies.

Teaching

Both directors teach at the Welsh School of Architecture in Cardiff and the University of Bath.

Accreditation

We are a ‘Royal Institute of British Architects’ Chartered Registered Practice.
